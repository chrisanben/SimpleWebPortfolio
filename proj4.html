Project: Hero Rush


Hero Rush is a co-developed flash game made in actionscript 3. It is a side scrolling game in which the player controls a "hero", in an arcade like game style, where you are to survive the longest hacking and slashing enemies or jumping over obstacles. The game had a small development cycle of less than a week and could be improved considerably. Graphics taken used for this game are of own creation or free to use license.


The mechanics of the game are founded on simple sidescrolling mechanics, in which the player avatar's location in the screen is unmovable in the X coordinates but to create and effect of scrolling the backdrop and enemies/obtascles move towards the player (left) and then dissapear of screen. This game was co-developed with Nolan Murphy.
